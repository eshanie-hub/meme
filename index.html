Roadmap

Leveraging novel and anti-fragile tokenomics to produce the greatest Meme DAO of all time


TOKENIZATION

Earn daily rewards in our native $CHAD token,increasing linearly with your NFT's rarity. The token is your ticket into our ecosystem -- emissions will be tightly controlled to both prevent hyperinflation and allow for the longevity of liquidity pools. Additionally, 45% of our trading fees from NFT secondary sales will be used to repurchase and burn our $CHAD token at random on a monthly basis.

BREEDING

Get access to sophisticated inter-collection and cross-collection staking and breeding mechanics funded exclusively with the $CHAD token. Offspring will reflect probability weighted trait inheritance and be airdropped to breeders at no additional cost. Those lucky enough to own rare and coveted NFTs will have the opportunity to loan them to other breeders at a hefty price -- subject to a breeding cooldown!

MEME FUND

Holders will organize to research and vote for the treasury to acquire a meme coin every month. Coins will be purchased with the remaining 45% of trading fees from secondary sales of our NFTs and will then be combined with the $CHAD token into liquidity pools, effectively allowing holders to exchange their $CHAD tokens and directing all trading fees back to the treasury.
